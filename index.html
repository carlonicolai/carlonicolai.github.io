I am Lecturer in Philosophy (Logic) at King's College London. I obtained my DPhil from the University of Oxford. Before King's, I was in Munich (MCMP) with a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Individual Fellowship, and in Utrecht with a VENI (NWO) grant. My research mostly focuses on the logic and the nature of the notions of truth, consequence, predication.

I am member of the London Group for Formal Philosophy.
